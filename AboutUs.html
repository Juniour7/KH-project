    Welcome to SDA Church Kahawa Wendani

    At SDA Church Kahawa Wendani, we are a vibrant and close-knit community dedicated to spreading the message of love, faith and service in accordance with the fundamental tenets of Seventh-Day Adventism. Our church, situated in the heart of Kahawa Wendani, is not just a place of worship but a home where individuals and families come together to grow spiritually.

    Our Mission

    Our Mission is to foster a community of believers who are pasionate about living out the teachings of Jesus Christ in harmony with the distinctive beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventists. We uphold the core tenets of the sventh-day adventist faith, including the observance of the Sabbath, imminent return of Christ and the importance of healthful living.

    Our History

    Established in [], SDA Church Kahawa Wendani has a rich history roted in faith and adherence to Seventh-day Adventist principles. Over the years, we have witnessed the transformative power of faith and we continue to be a beacon of hope for the residents of Kahawa Wendani.

    Service and Programs

    Join us for our uplifting worship services, angaging Bible studies and various community outreach programs that reflect our commitment to SDA values. From inspiring sermons to dynmaic youth activities we integrate Seventh-day Adventist tenets into our activities to nuture spiritual growth.

    Seventh-Day Adventist Tenets

    1. Sabbath Observance: We honor and keep the Sabbath, a day of rest and worship in accordance with the fourth commandment.
    2. Second coming of Christ: We anticipate the imminent return of Jesus Christ, seeking to live in readiness and anticipation of His glorious advent.
    3. Healthful Living: Recognizing our bodies as temples of the Holy Spirit, we emphasize healthful living through principles such as a vegeterian diet and abstaining from harmful substances.
