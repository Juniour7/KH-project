    • A. Key Benevolence Guidlines

    1. Dependents
      1. Children of the registered members who are below 18 years, or those above 18 years who are in school or college.
      2. Biological parent or registered guardian of the fund members
      3. Any other special case that may be considered by the committe and approved by the special church business meeting of Fund members through a simple majority vote.
    2. Registration Fee of KES.500 is applicable for new members (Mpesa No. 0114220367)
    3. Contributions: Members are required to make contributions every time a member is bereaved.
    4. Benefit: The committee shall give a total of KES.50,000 (Fifty Thousand), to the bereaved family. The bereaved member should not have defaulted thr past three (3) consecutive cases.

    B. Member Details

    Mission

    The Mission of the Seventh-day Adventist Church is to proclaim to all the people the Everlasting Gospel in the context of the Three Angels Message of Revelation 14:6-12 leading them to accept Jesus as personal Saviour and to unite with His church, and nurturing them in preparation for His soon return.
